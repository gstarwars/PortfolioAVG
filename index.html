About me

I am an AUT design student working across animation, VFX, game design and extended reality. My projects often combine playful concepts with strong visual identity, and I enjoy developing work that feels imaginative but still clear in its storytelling.

I am especially interested in how animation and interactive media can overlap — whether through immersive environments, stylised motion, or personal film work that uses strong atmosphere and design to create emotional impact.

Study direction

This portfolio was prepared to support exchange and course review. It highlights both practical production work and broader creative interests in digital storytelling, animation craft, game-focused thinking and emerging media.

My goal is to keep building a body of work that is visually expressive, technically capable and open to experimentation across different creative forms.
